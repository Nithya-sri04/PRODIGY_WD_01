a word about us

Nithuzz restaurant opened on Wednesday 2004. Chef / Owner Nithyasri began baking pies and selling them to restaurants and her neighbors out of a small kitchen.

It was opened at the corner of Nehru street of Thousand lights. Today, Chennai's beloved restaurant and pie shop celebrates 18years of classic, made from scratch Indian cooking
